# Convex Logs: Useful but Insufficient Alone

**Confidence:** 0.78

**Sources:**
- https://docs.convex.dev/dashboard/deployments/logs
- https://docs.convex.dev/functions/debugging
- https://docs.convex.dev/production/integrations/log-streams

**Summary:**
Convex logs and log streams are useful operational tools, but logs alone are a weak source of durable causal history for agentic debugging, especially where structured event correlation and replay diagnostics are needed.
